Audio used to be full of gaslighters. I remember walking into a store in 2015 and being told that if I couldn't hear the difference between a $500 cable and a coat hanger, my ears were "undeveloped."

Here is the thing about the industry: Confusion is profitable.

The "Burn-in" Myth

Let's talk about mechanical burn-in vs. brain burn-in. While mechanical suspension changes exist, 99% of what you perceive as "opening up" is just your brain stopping its rejection of a new sound signature.

"If you have to listen to it for 200 hours to like it, maybe you just have Stockholm Syndrome, not good headphones."

I ran a frequency response test on my new IEMs right out of the box, and again after 100 hours of pink noise. The difference? Statistically insignificant.
